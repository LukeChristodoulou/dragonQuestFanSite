The Game

Dragon Quest V: Hand of the Heavenly Bride is a Japanese fantasy role-playing video game and the fifth installment in the Dragon Quest video game series. The game takes place over roughly thirty years of the main character's life, from when he is born through to when he gets married and has a family.
